About Advanced Word Counter

Advanced Word Counter is a powerful text analysis tool built with Django that provides comprehensive statistics about your text content.

Features

  • Word Count: Get both total and unique word counts
  • Character Analysis: See character counts with and without spaces
  • Sentence Detection: Automatic sentence counting
  • Average Word Length: Understand the complexity of your text
  • Reading Time: Estimate how long it takes to read your text
  • Frequency Analysis: See which words appear most often
  • Smart Word Processing: Handles punctuation and capitalization intelligently

Use Cases

  • Writers checking article or essay length
  • Students meeting assignment word requirements
  • Content creators analyzing readability
  • SEO professionals optimizing content
  • Researchers analyzing text patterns

Technology Stack

  • Backend: Django (Python web framework)
  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3
  • Text Processing: Python regular expressions

Privacy

Your text is processed locally and is not stored or transmitted anywhere. All analysis happens in real-time on the server and results are displayed immediately.
